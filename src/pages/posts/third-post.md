Hello world! I wanted to give you all a quick update on my goals and share some thoughts about where I'm at in my current life journey.

## Thoughts on Privacy

Over the past decade, I've learned that honesty has always been my best policy. However, there's a fine line between being honest and oversharing. As someone who's learned this the hard way, I now understand the importance of a balanced approach to sharing information.

I've been contemplating whether I should redact information from my previous blog posts. After much reflection, I've decided to leave them up for two reasons:

1. **Authenticity**: I want to meet new people who will see me for who I'm trying to become. By sharing certain details about myself, even those that don't paint me in the best light, I'm opening myself up to genuine connections. Staying in my shell for the rest of my life won't help me grow as a person. I believe I'll eventually meet people who see my potential and share my values.

2. **Reclaiming Power**: Deleting my old posts would give my old friends exactly what they want. I finally have the agency to change my life's direction, and I won't give in to those who don't have my best interests at heart.

That said, I won't be sharing every aspect of myself. Unfortunately, experiences with family and friends have taught me to always watch my back. There's a balance between telling people EVERYTHING and deleting all social media. I'm still figuring out what that looks like, but I'm a student of life. I'll be navigating this line for years to come. On the bright side, I believe I'm now a better judge of character than I used to be.

## Scaling Back

I initially planned to work on five big projects this month, but I've realized I might be stretching myself too thin. I've decided to focus on four projects instead. The PyTorch project is a bit more than I can handle right now. Even four projects is ambitious for one month, so I'll also be scaling back for August.

Networking is still a priority, but I've reconsidered my approach. Instead of aiming for three meetups, I'm going to focus on attending just one. This feels like a more manageable way to continue my progress without feeling overwhelmed or stressed.

## Going Forward

I'm genuinely excited about my current direction. I won't let anyone—including myself—stop me from reaching even greater heights. I've never been this productive or enthusiastic, which tells me I'm on the right track to finally living up to my full potential.

I'm also really enjoying writing these blog posts, so you can expect to see more in the coming weeks. It's become a great way for me to reflect on my progress and share my journey with you all.

Thanks again for reading and for your continued support. Here's to growth, self-discovery, and the adventures ahead!
