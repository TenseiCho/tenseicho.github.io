# My Roadmap for July

Hey everyone! I've got some big plans for July, and I'm excited to share them with you. My focus is going to be on three main areas: Projects, Networking, and Fun. Let's dive in!

## Projects: Pushing My Boundaries

I've been on a roll lately with projects like the r/a/dio Firefox extension, Andrew the Discord Bot, WEBP-PNG converter, and my portfolio website. But I'm not stopping there. July is going to be all about pushing myself further. Here's what I've got lined up:

1. **r/a/dio Android App**: Building on the success of the Firefox extension, I'm diving into Flutter to create an Android app. It's more about mastering the Flutter structure than figuring out the logic this time.

2. **TenseiRhythm**: I've always wanted to make my own video game, and I'm finally taking the plunge. I'm creating a rhythm game from scratch. It's ambitious, but I'm ready for the challenge!

3. **Taro.Quest**: This is going to be a website built with Three.js. I'm still firming up the details, but I'm excited to flex my 3D web development muscles.

4. **Taro.VA**: Ever wanted your own personal voice assistant? That's exactly what I'm building here. It's a project I've been dreaming about for a while.

5. **PyTorch Project**: I'm diving into machine learning! The goal is to create an AI that can play either Super Auto Pets or osu. We'll see how it goes!

It's an ambitious list, but I'm determined to make significant progress on these projects throughout July.

## Networking: Stepping Out of My Comfort Zone

This month, I'm making a concerted effort to expand my professional network. It's time to get out there and meet some brilliant minds in the tech world. My goals are:

- Attend a Tech Meetup
- Participate in a Hackathon
- Join a CTF (Capture The Flag) Event

I'll be honest, this part scares me a bit. I don't have many friends in the tech world, but that's exactly why I need to do this. It's not just about career opportunities; it's about connecting with people who share my passions and drive for self-improvement.

## Fun: Balancing Work with Play

All work and no play? Not on my watch! I'm setting some personal goals to ensure I'm not just growing professionally, but also enjoying life:

1. **Explore New Games**: I love video games, but I've been stuck in a rut. Time to try some new titles and broaden my gaming horizons.

2. **Solo Restaurant Adventures**: This is a big one for me. I want to go out to eat at new restaurants... by myself. It's something I've always wanted to do but kept talking myself out of. My goal is to try three new restaurants this month. No more gaslighting myself into staying home!

3. **Outdoor Activities**: I want to spend more time outside, doing stuff without worrying about what others think. It's time to embrace the world beyond my comfort zone.

## Wrapping Up

This roadmap for July is all about growth - in my skills, my network, and my personal life. It's ambitious, sure, but I'm excited about the challenge. I'm looking forward to learning more about myself in the process.

Stay tuned for an update in August (which happens to be my birthday month)! I'll let you know how it all went down.

Here's to a month of coding, connecting, and personal challenges. Wish me luck!
