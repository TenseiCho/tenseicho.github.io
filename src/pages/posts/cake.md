As much as I enjoy being in the new city, can't I have my cake and eat it too. Why am I even out here to begin with? The timeline was split. There is another world where I would be in a different city playing a nostalgic game with my best friend in the whole wide world. I would be able to meet all my online friends that I had known for over ten years. I was so excited for all the best times ahead. 

Well, that didn’t happen.

What happened was I betrayed my best friend for the sake of something I thought greater. I wanted to build a new foundation for the discord of my friend group that I had. I didn’t want to be associated with racist, transphobic people anymore. I couldn’t bare to speak to the person who I had loved with on terms of amicability. I was a coward. I wanted my cake and I wanted to eat it too. Well, I did eat it all right. I ate well. I played God with people who I had trusted for a long time and hurt a lot of people in the process. I betrayed the friend group by dividing people and taking the heat for what would make me lose sleep for months on end. 

I bled for what I thought was a clever cause. However, my worst sin became true as I slowly realized over the most painful two years of my entire existence that I not only had destroyed myself in the process, I was just a reflection of who I was. I surrounded myself with assholes, because I was an asshole. I surrounded myself with people pleasers, because I was one. I was the problem. I had to fix it. Hopefully, with this blog post, I can close this chapter on the past ten years of my life and I can move forward with who I want to be.

<h2 style="text-align: center;">Online Persona</h2>

When I was in high school, I played a lot of online games. I created an online persona that I've used for the past decade. I'm not sure how much backstory I want to give but basically, I was a spoiled, entitled person. That's what my best friend said to me as I was being an asshole to him.

Because I didn't have a social life growing up due to personal issues, I chose to be myself online. Around the people I associated with at the time, I created an online group. It's still around if you want to join a dead group, but a few years ago it was quite popular. Looking back, I unintentionally created a cult-like following. Everyone was vying for status in the group. I was playing God even back then. Charisma & an Ego is a very dangerous drug. I don't want to go into detail about what that was all about because that's not the point of this blog post. It's just to give some detail into who I was. 

People give me a lot of shit for being born into an upper middle-class family. I wish my life was that easy. I've been through so much shit I could write fifteen more blogs on stuff but that's not what this is about. I do say I was spoiled because I didn't have to work at all. I basically went to school & cheated. God, it actually disgusts me writing about myself. It's like I am finally waking up from an eternal slumber of moral depravity. 

Too long didn't read, I sat around inside all day scared of the outside world. I spent all my time online being a hedonist and pretending like I could get an easy 6 figure job outside of community college. Gluttony, Sloth & Wrath were my favorite sins. I was also very angry all the time but I could never quite put my finger on why.

Suffice to say, I need to get to the point of this blog before I start rambling. 

<h2 style="text-align: center;">Recent Past</h2>

A while back, I was in college studying computer science. I finally got a taste of independence. I was going to school, working out & hanging out with friends online. Life was going well. However, there was an uneasy feeling in the back of my head. There was a person I had known since just after high school. I met them while playing an online game. We became friends, but our relationship was complicated.

At some point, we started to spend a lot more time together. We played cooperative games together. It was funny how much they complained but continued to play with me. I appreciated that they apologized for complaining most of the way through, but it was nice to spend time with someone who wanted to spend time with me.

Eventually, we would spend time together doing things couples would do. The problem was I was a people pleaser. If I wasn't, I would have clarified what was happening because there was an extremely blurred line. However, this was the first time since my mom had passed away that I had ever been given attention like that. It was cathartic. 

All the while, I was slowly realizing that surrounding myself with people who don’t respect other people was probably not a good idea. The person who I was spending time with was an avid Kiwifarms enjoyer. Kiwifarms is an online forum to harass & dox mentally unwell people. When I was in school, I was terrified of being shamed online for the world to see because I did something embarrassing. 

Ironic, isn’t it?

Eventually, I cared very deeply about my friend group and I wanted to move forward without being surrounded by people who don’t respect other people. At some point, I made the choice to ban all the people from the discord who I deemed undesirable. These are people I have also known for a long time. It was an incredibly difficult decision for me to make and I consulted people who I had trusted for the longest time on this. They said it was a good idea and so I did. I just want to say how fucking disgusting of a human being I was for doing it the way I did. I don’t blame anyone after that point for losing all respect for me. However, the deed was done including the person I cared about the most. I betrayed them for the sake of an empire of dust.

<h2 style="text-align: center;">2023</h2>

If you guys are reading this from the old discord groups, thanks for leaving me alone. I will never come back to the discord and I would suggest you stop reading here. I don’t have anything nice to say going forward. Except for a certain someone.

The pain was so unbearable, I was truly alone. Well, I probably would have been better alone in hindsight because I had someone who I shared all these secrets with. I was in so much pain & agony I eventually became numb. I needed someone to talk to because I was alone. I NEEDED anything to save me from what was the hardest pain I had ever had to deal with in my entire life. It’s funny that someone online would have more of an impact on my life then seeing my mother pass away before my very eyes.

I trusted someone with a lot of information because they were my best friend. However, when I told that person that they couldn't come to my family because of drama, it became so clear looking in the mirror that this person was a reflection of me. Fat, people-pleasing, angry, selfish, backstabbing. It was me! I was abhorrent. I am getting ahead of myself but suffice to say I never wanted to ever associate with this person again. However, they loved me. They loved the idea of me. They hated themself and were looking for a way out. So, I was stuck with them especially since I didn't want to keep banishing people I didn't want in my life. 

The year ended with an annual gaming event where we all get together and watch new games being advertised. However, something was eating away at the back of my heart. What you might not know was there was a lot of cross-communication between the people who I had banned and the people still in the group. I don't want to make any guesses, but from our shared online spaces constantly being disrupted, I'm sure they were still upset with me. I don't blame them at all.

However, the vibes were off. I could tell. I'm not that stupid or so I tell myself. The feeling that my friends were talking a bunch of shit behind my back was eating away at me. There is no way that my friends who I loved & cared for would betray me? Right?

<h2 style="text-align: center;">2024</h2>

I'm still in shock about what happened two years later. I don't know if anyone in the friend group actually cared at all about me because no one ever asked about how I was doing or what I was up to except the people pleaser. It felt like being the biggest clown at the circus was slowly coming to a close. I can't believe I hurt them but I still feel like I was justified. It was also apparent to me that something needed to change but I don't know what. It was eating away at me but all different directions. I was back at my abuser's house as they lied to me about everything.

My course of action was to ignore them because at a certain point engaging with people that will hurt you is just playing on there level. I was still not working either. So, I was just relying on money that I didn't earn which was very obvious as people admonished me & made me feel guilty all the time for that very reason. I don't mind telling people now because I'm currently working on finding a job, projects & on my body. Back then, I was just a fat lazy NEET so of course no one respected me. 

It was apparent to me that no one liked or respected me as people that I genuinely loved disrespected me all the time. The only people who kept me around were liars & people pleasers. Great company all around! 

The final event was going to a gaming convention. However, I do need to give some context. With me banning "all" the problematic people, it became clear that everyone who was a good liar kept that going except one person. That person showed up and it was clearly a lack of respect for what I had done. That person was unironically repugnant and it made me realize that everything I had done was for nothing.

I appreciate the honesty when they asked me why I didn't like them and I said it was because they were racist. They told me "Well so & so is also racist". Yeah, I should have known better. Anyways, the gaming convention happens and we all meet up. Even before the incident happened the sinking feeling was happening the entire time. Something wasn't right. Why was this person in the friend group? Why does no one seem to have a problem with them?

Well, the incident happened. Basically, I was in the wrong. I was not sober and I was very uncomfortable around this person. We were playing a game like apples to apples where you have to make up scenarios and I made a scene. If I had the ability to realize I was a vibe ruiner I would have just left but I didn't. Everyone hated me that day and we all headed back. No one talked to me the entire night. I knew I was in the wrong. When we got back, I could hear two of my friends who I had trusted talk so much shit behind my back. When I got out, I talked to one of them hoping I could engage in a good faith discussion and they just flat out lied to me. They also went into the room with that person. 

These people are not my friends.

<h2 style="text-align: center;">TenseiCho</h2>

I just can't stop dying & being rebirthed. My Isekai! I removed everyone I knew and realized I need to start over. The people are never going to change, I need to change. I never should have divided people according to my own value system. It's hard to know how I should of went about things but the person I am now would never have associated with them to begin with.

Let me start off by giving out some apologizes to someone who I hurt the most.

I am sorry for betraying your trust, not just once, but twice.

I am sorry for yelling at you. 

I am sorry for being a shitty person who couldn't tell you how I really felt at the time.

I am sorry for calling you a bitch.

Despite it all, you meant the world to me and yet here I am. Alone but not unhappy. I finally figured out who I want to be and the only person stopping me is me, myself & I.

Please understand that if that you never rejected me, I would never have become the person I know I can be. That's why my name is Tensei. I don't think I would have ever left my room of my abuser's house. I would never of realized the errors of my ways. I never would have moved across the entire country to finally start living my life.

You saved my life.

It's a butterfly effect. That's why my name is Cho. You showed me a life that I never thought would be possible because I was in a bubble of my own misery. I have nothing to give you other than giving thanks where it's due.

Thank you for telling me no when no one else would.

Thank you for being an honest person.

Thank you for showing me what love really looks like.

Finally. 

And I mean this from the bottom of my heart.

Thank you for being a part of my life.
