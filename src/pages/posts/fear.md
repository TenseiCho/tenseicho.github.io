Hello, it's been a hot minute since I last wrote a blog post. I'll be honest, I very much like it that way. Being able to enjoy life without living in a panopticon of my own choosing is very much how I like to start living life. The past 5 months I have been able to create a website, web extension & mobile app. Now that I have some projects under my belt (and more to come), I decided to take a giant risk which I will explore more in my next blog post. In the meantime, I wanted to talk a little bit about fear and the best & worst aspects of it.  
  
## Worst:  
  
I lived my life as a negative person for the past ten years of my life. I decided that for the next ten years I will be doing the opposite and see where my life takes me. In that aspect, I will be discussing the worst parts of fear first. The worst part of fear is ceasing to exist for all of eternity. You could make that same argument for the best as well but you will never know what the other side of fear looked like because you lived in a world of 'what ifs' and never chose happiness instead. Kinda like that bird in the image, you only saw what you perceived as being your own happiness. That, to me, is the worst part of fear: never taking action and dying never knowing what could have been.  
  
I'm so glad to have had actions happen to me because I constantly was living like the bird for a long time. Yeah, it was terrifying to see the actual truth of who I was because I loved living in a false reality. I could have betrayed myself for a lot longer and I'm glad I came out the other side stronger & more resilient.

## Best:

Notice I didn't mention dying in the worst? When I was on the REDACTED, I knew at some point anything bad could have happened. However, I was the one making that decision and at any point in time I could have turned back. At one point, I did almost die and my journey could have ended at the start. George Addair said it best, "Everything you've ever wanted is on the other side of fear". Now that I have made it, I couldn't agree more if I tried.  
  
Think about one thing that you are always scared about and do it. You will be surprised what you can do when you become your own best friend. Anyways, next blog is going to be a long one and I'm super excited to go over it.

Catch you on the flip side!
