## To my mother

It has taken me a long time to reconcile with the notion that you no longer reside in this realm of existence. I'm not delusional enough to think I will ever see you again. Writing this is more for myself than anything else. I have lost touch with my own humanity and became something of a disgusting amalgamation of gluttony, envy, sloth, wrath & worst of all, pride.

This is not the life I want you to see me in if you were to ever see me. I want you to see me rise to heights that no person ever thought possible. Not only for myself, but for my future wife & progeny. I will aim for the next ten years to accomplish that task. I want you to know that your gift will no longer be used for hedonistic purposes. Instead, I will build a castle & a kingdom for my family to be truly proud of.

I miss you so much and if there is a heaven, I pray you look down upon me for the next ten years with amusement instead of disappointment.

Thank you & I love you,

* Jay Ciretose

## Memento Mori

Rewriting this portion after dealing with someone who should have stayed in the past. I never thought I would ever write this, but I am forgiving myself for what I have done. The past has already been written and to think that bringing someone back wasn’t going to bring pain was delusional. I want to continue to be myself, but I need the people from which I was a horrible person to stay in that which was already been written.

The person I am now is still silly, flamboyant and a “bit” obnoxious. However, what’s done is done and deluding ourselves that things can change is beyond contempt in my books. Granted, I can’t read very well. As much as I would love my situationship with my previous friends to change and for us to get back together and have rainbows & butterflies. That is losing the plot.

The people that I care so deeply about were who I was at the time that it was being written and for who they were at that point in their time. We must continue onwards despite our wishes for something to be different. What we are doing in this instance is betraying our future friends & family. I don’t know who they are, but I know that if I told them what I did the past couple of days they would probably force me to sleep on the couch. And as a man, I would have no problems with that. The couch is nice, after all.

You must understand, the only things I can think of from the past have been nothing but genuine regret. I stayed in my room and did nothing but play “god” because I was hurt for most of my childhood & teenage life. I never once thought to properly socialize with the outside world or see perspectives different from my own. I never once thought of losing weight so I could experience what life could be like if I wasn’t morbidly obese. I never once thought to put a modicum of effort into finding work so I wouldn’t end up in this experience that I am. In the end, it was my own dam fault for being the way I was and I’m glad people stopped respecting me. I didn’t respect myself so why should anyone else do the same for me?

I was a broken child and for that, I will forgive myself. I want to be healthy.I want to be wealthy. I want friends who want what’s best for me & vice versa. I want a spouse who will love me for me & vice versa. and most of all.

I want to be happy.
