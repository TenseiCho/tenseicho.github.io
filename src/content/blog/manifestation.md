I have recently come to terms with who I used to be. The problem I am currently facing is, "who am I?". I feel like 2024 was getting hell away from a horrible situation online & offline. It was also about healing from the trauma that I was inflicted with, and I inflicted upon others. Those five months of meditation have saved my life & I am eternally grateful I got out when I did. Going into the next year will be mainly about rediscovering who I am.

I would be lying if the apparition of Carolina didn’t somehow manifest itself in certain moments. I would be very perplexed by the end of next year if that shell of my former self remains. I have given myself some semblance of an understanding because I used to be so negative & hard on myself. Now, I’m a lot nicer to myself & I understand that things will not always be easy. Giving it time and being a lot more peaceful in my approach to life will be the ticket to success. I could be wrong; I have been before. However, I like to think that this current trajectory I am on will lead me to roads that I have only ever dreamed of. 

On the last blog post, I mentioned being healthy, wealthy, having good friends & a good partner. Going to go into more detail on this post.

Health is something that I have always neglected and that's something I want to rectify in the coming years. The heaviest I weighed was around 360 pounds. As for writing this, I am currently 273. I am still a long way away from being 170 pounds but by the end of next year I should be around that point. That means eating a low carb high fat diet & fasting for the rest of the day. Also playing a ton of Beat Saber & other sports eventually. Obesity is a disease and I’m going to fix it. I was sick & now that I’m in a better place mentally, it’s time to reflect on that physically. 

Wealth is important as well; it would be a good idea for me to eventually get a job, but I know my history is kind of trash. I've spent a lot of time trying to build something from nothing and I'm happy with how my projects are turning out. My goal is to finish at least ten software projects, ten 3DCG models & ten main YT channel videos before the summer of next year. At that point, I would like to think that I would be employed for a remote job. We shall see but nonetheless I want to start making my own money so people can stop shaming me for being lazy.

Speaking of friends, that is another aspect that I need to be careful of. I need to be more cautious of the people who surround my inner circle. I want people who are trying to be better people inside & out. I want people who are respectful of others even if they don't necessarily agree with certain viewpoints. That means I need to do a better job communicating my thoughts & feelings with others in a healthier way. Something that has always been a point of contention with my past self. It’s always easy to just banish people who you disagree with. That doesn’t fix the issue. To be a great leader, you need to inspire others. I didn’t inspire a whole lot of confidence in anything in the past two years. My past self has only made lifelong enemies doing what I did. I’m a lover, not a fighter.

Again, time does heal all wounds, whether we want it to or not. I know that I have had a lot of issues in the past, still do if you ask certain people. But that shouldn’t stop you or I from living the best life that we can. Learning from our mistakes is what makes us human, Afterall.

At the end of the day, I also want to be happy. I'm going to be alone for most of the time and if I can't find happiness in that it’s just a major skill issue that needs to be fixed. The world is massive & I'm incredibly lucky to be alive. I should never, ever, take that for granted again. Even if I never find love. I want to live my life to the fullest until I'm six feet under.

And on that note, dear reader, I will see you all into the next year.
